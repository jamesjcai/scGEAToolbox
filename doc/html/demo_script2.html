HVG analysis with single data X

[X,genelist]=sc_readfile('example_data/GSM3044891_GeneExp.UMIs.10X1.txt');

[X,genelist]=sc_selectg(X,genelist,3,1);
% Normalize data with DESeq method
Xn=sc_norm(X,'type','deseq');
[T]=sc_hvg(Xn,genelist,true,true);

NOTE: Genes with dropout rate > 0.95 are excluded. scGEAToolbox controls for the variance-mean relationship of gene expression. scGEAToolbox considers three sample statistics of each gene: expression mean, coefficient of variation, and dropout rate. After normalization, it fits a spline function based on piece-wise polynomials to model the relationship among the three statistics, and calculates the distance between each geneis observed statistics to the fitted 3D spline surface. Genes with larger distances are ranked higher for feature selection.
